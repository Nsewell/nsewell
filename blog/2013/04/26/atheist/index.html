Why I'm an Atheist

Apr 26th, 2013

![submission-umbrellas.gif]

There are between 38,000 and 41,000 Christian denominations, more than the 33,829 sentences in the KJV. Some of these denominations are the same movement in different countries, but the figures show a remarkable diversity of opinion not just on liturgy but on scripture and theology.

Protestants maintain that the only thing necessary for belief is scripture alone (Sola Scriptura). If this is correct, and all Catholics are in error, then why didn’t Jesus give his disciples a printing press?
