Polarisation Getting Worse

Jul 7th, 2014

The Pew Forum finds that the American voting elite are becoming more polarised on the political wings, with the parties appealing more strongly to consistently progressive/conservative voters cloistered into their own media niche.

Claire Lehmann argues that opinionated political bloggers are driving a partisan divide in Australia

The sensible middle-ground of politics is being lost in a sea of reactive, frothing debate. And what is to blame? Claire Lehmann says it is page-view journalism.
