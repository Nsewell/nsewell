Crocodiles in Slow Motion

Feb 3rd, 2014

The Superbowl ads this year seem completely underwhelming, all the good ones have already been ‘leaked’ on youtube. This ad for tourism in the Northern Territory shits on them all, watch it in HD:

Fishing for Crocodiles - Slow Motion
Thanks to http://www.crocosauruscove.com in Darwin, Australia. You’re able to feed, handle, and swim with crocodiles. This is part of the feeding using a fishing rod. Quite a unique experience. - As you can see they jump pretty high out the water. I didn’t know they could actually jump completely out of water. Filming these animals in slow motion is just beautiful. Something you rarely see. On a technical side of things. This was all filmed using a Sony fs700 with a 18-200mm 3.5-5f lens. Shot at 480 frames a second. I borrowed the camera from Sony in Hong Kong to test, and honestly I love it so much. You can capture such amazing detail in slow motion. If you’re going to shoot slow motion it’s the cheapest camera for an indie cinematographer. I wasn’t paid to say any of this, Sony just wanted some footage. Maybe I’ll buy the camera soon. http://pro.sony.com/bbsc/ssr/product-NEXFS700U/ I’m on Facebook http://www.facebook.com/allandx I’m on Twitter http://www.twitter.com/daxon I’m on Instagram http://www.instagram.com/daxon Thanks
