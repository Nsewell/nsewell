Crocodiles in Slow Motion

Feb 3rd, 2014

The Superbowl ads this year seem completely underwhelming, all the good ones have already been ‘leaked’ on youtube. This ad for tourism in the Northern Territory shits on them all, watch it in HD:

Fishing for Crocodiles - Slow Motion
Thanks to http://www.crocosauruscove.com in Darwin, Australia. You can feed crocodiles, swim with crocodiles, and get really up close to them. Feeding them using a fishing rod is a really unique experience, as you can see they jump pretty high out the water. I didn’t know they could actually jump completely out of water. The slow motion gives you really good detail in the crocodiles movement. Something you rarely see. On a technical side of things. This was all filmed using a Sony fs700 with a 18-200mm 3.5-5f lens. Shot at 480 frames a second. I borrowed the camera from Sony to test and love it’s ability to capture slow motion. If you’re going to shoot slow motion it’s the cheapest camera for an indie cinematographer. I wasn’t paid to say any of this, Sony just wanted some footage. Maybe I’ll buy the camera soon. http://pro.sony.com/bbsc/ssr/product-NEXFS700U/ I’m on Facebook http://www.facebook.com/allandx I’m on Twitter http://www.twitter.com/daxon I’m on Instagram http://www.instagram.com/daxon Thanks
