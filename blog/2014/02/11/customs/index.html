Customs Propaganda Broadcasts Suffering in Immigration Detention

Feb 11th, 2014

Government propagandists for Customs are now broadacasting that asylum seekers will suffer physical and mental harm in detention camps:

The Australian public is being kept in the dark like never before about the number of boat arrivals and the conditions of the detention camps. The public is allowed to know that the camps cost $1.5 billion each year to operate, but the government has effectively denied Australian journalists any real coverage of the conditions in the camps. The politically motivated expulsion of a judge from Nauru before an inquiry into their detention camps is another way in which secrecy is being maintained.

The Abbott government claims that they have a mandate to “stop the boats.” It’s unclear if the people that gave them that mandate understand what is happening with their money, in their name.
